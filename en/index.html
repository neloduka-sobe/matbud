About Us

Over 25 years of experience in fire safety

Our History

Founded in 1998, Matbud Systemy Ppoż. sp. z o.o. has grown from a small local business into a leading provider of fire safety solutions. With a commitment to excellence and client satisfaction, we have built a reputation for reliability and expertise in the industry.

Our Mission

Our mission is to protect lives and property by delivering the highest quality fire safety systems and services. We strive to stay at the forefront of fire safety technology and to provide solutions that meet the unique needs of each client.
